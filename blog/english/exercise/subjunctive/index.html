3. I failed all my finals. I ___ studying the whole weekend instead of 
   partying. (2023年11)
   A. should have been  本应该做某事但实际没做

   C. must have been    对过去的肯定  一定已经做了某事
   D. might have been   对过去可能性的猜测   或许已经做了某事
